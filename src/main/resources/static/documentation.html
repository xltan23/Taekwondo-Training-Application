Legend
Duration: A duration score is calculated according to the time spent performing a particular workout. This calculation is dependent on how intense the workout is.
Repetition: A repetition score is calculated according to the number of reps performed. This calculation is dependent on how intense the workout is.
Sets: Number of sets performed for particular workout.
Intensity level: The intensity level the workout is class under.
Intensity score: An intensity score takes into account the duration/repetition score, no. of sets performed and an intensity-dependent multiplier to generate final score.

Upon hover, each image overlay displays the name of workout, the intensity level and a brief description of the workout. Intensity level is class into 4 levels:   Low   Moderate   High   Extreme   This colour will also be reflected in the cell containing the image and dropdown options.
   
Dropdown options allow users to select specific number of duration/repetition per set. Each specific duration/repetition has a score tied to it and scores may not necessarily be proportionate to the number. Higher end number or intensity yield exponentially higher scores thereby making it more rewarding to perform those for shorter workout sessions. In the example above, 10 just means 10s or 10 reps. 12/S means 12 per side (On each leg). XX-XX is usually indicative of a package drill. 20-15 may mean 20 reps of kick and 15s hold. 1/5 mass means the weights used is 1/5 of the user's body mass. If workout requires only two weights (eg. chest press), 1/5 mass is the mass of each weights.
Poomsae Box
STRETCH & STATICS (15 Workouts)
Supported Wall Package: P1 - Front/Side Kick Preparation. Partner presses knee and ankle against user. P2 - Front/Side Kick Raise. Partner lift extended leg over shoulder. P3 - Front/Side Kick Supported kick. Partner hold onto user's knee in mid-air as pivot. User performs mid-air kicks. 20-10 refers to 20 seconds hold and 10 repetition mid-air kicks
Half Splits Package: Pigeon stretch - User sits on folded front leg Half-Frog stretch - Side split but with one side folded
Splits Package: Standard side split and 2 front Splits
Wall Splits: Front/Side kick full extension against the wall. May require partner's support.
Leg Swings: Swing leg as high as possible in turn/side kick style.
Quick Half Push-ups: Rapid fist push-ups. Only half push.
Reverse Fist Push-ups: Push-ups with fist facing outwards.
Quick Wall Squats: Hold on the wall and perform quick half squats.
Single-Leg Wall Squats: Hold on the wall and perform quick single-leg half squats.
Wall Sit: Sit against the wall.
Standard squats: Full standing squats.
Jump squats: Jump to a squat and jump from the squat.
Side Sit-ups: Perform sit-up while lying on the side.
Crunch Thrust: Sit and both leg thrust.
Plank Package: Front and side planks on both sides.
TECHNIQUE DRILL (20 Workouts)
Alternate Punching (Horse): In horse-riding stance, perform punches in group setting.
Low/High/Inward Block (Forward): In forward stance, perform any of above blocks in static position.
Single/Double Knife Block (Back): In back stance, perform any of above blocks in static position.
Punching/Blocks (Resistance): Resistance band one end strapped to punching/blocking fist one end held by partner.
Punching/Block (Alternating Stance) Performed in 2 steps. Step 1 - Back foot shifts to align with front foot, body weight shift over to that back foot. Prepare pulling fist. Step 2 - Execute the punch/block and shift front foot back into forward/back stance.
Basic Movements: 1st Sequence: Low Block+Easy Stance => Punch+Forward Stance => Inward block+Back Stance => Double Knife Block+Cat Stance => Double Fist Rib Punch+Tiger Stance => Keumgang Crane => Ilyeo Spear Knife+Reverse Crane Stance => Ojang Backfist Stamp => Pyongwon Elbow Cross 2nd Sequence: Hammerfist+L-Stance => Mountain Block+Horseriding Stance => Paljang Modified Forward
Movement Package: Advancing forward with: Punch+Forward Stance, Low Block+Forward Stance, High Block+Forward Stance, Inward Block+Forward Stance, Double Knife+Back Stance.
Movement Package (Weighted): Strapped weights to the belt. Advancing forward with: Punch+Forward Stance, Low Block+Forward Stance, High Block+Forward Stance, Inward Block+Forward Stance, Double Knife+Back Stance.
Poomsae by Steps: Perform each step with number of repetition set. (i.e. Step 1 X-times, Step 2 X-times, Step 3 X-times...)
Poomsae by Parts: Perform each set of movement with number of repetition set. (i.e. Step 1-6 X-times...)
Poomsae by Components:
IlJang - Step 5-6/11-12 repeat, Step 7-8/9-10 repeat
EeJang - Step 1-2/3-4 repeat, Step 5-6/11-12 repeat
SamJang - Step 1-2/3-4 repeat, Step 5-6/11-12 repeat, Step 7-8/9-10 repeat
SaJang - Step 1-2/3-4 repeat, Step 5-6/13-14 repeat, Step 9-10/11-12 repeat
OJang - Step 1-2/3-4 repeat, Step 5-8 repeat, Step 9-10/11-12 repeat, Step 15-16/17-18 repeat, Step 19-20 repeat
YukJang - Step 1-2/3-4 repeat, Step 5-land/11-land, Step 16-19 repeat
ChilJang - Step 1-2/3-4 repeat, Step 5-6/12-13 repeat, Step 14-16/17-19 repeat, Step 20-25 repeat
PalJang - Step 1-3/18-19 repeat, Step 5-6/7-8 repeat (start 4), Step 9-11 repeat, Step 12-14/15-17 repeat, Step 20-23/24-27 repeat
Koryo - Step 1-4/5-8 repeat, Step 9-12 repeat, Step 13-15 repeat, Step 16-25 repeat, Step 27-30 repeat
Keumgang - Step 1-4 repeat, Step 5-7 repeat, Step 11-15/21-25 repeat (connect using Step 9-10 & 16-17)
Taebaek - Step 1-2/3-4/23-24/25-26 repeat, Step 5-8 repeat, Step 9-18 repeat, Step 19-21 repeat
Pyongwon - Step 4-6/13-15 repeat, Step 7-11/16-20 repeat, Step 12/21 repeat
Sipjin - Step 1-4/6-9 repeat, Step 14-20 repeat, Step 21-24 repeat, Step 25-28 repeat
Full Poomsae: Perform entire Poomsae in regular rhythm and timing.
Slow Motion Poomsae: Perform entire Poomsae in slow motion timing (5 counts per step).
Slow Motion Poomsae by Parts: Perform by parts Poomsae (4-6 steps) in slow motion timing (5 counts per step).
Full Poomsae (Weighted): Perform entire Poomsae with weights strapped on belt.
Poomsae by Parts (Weighted): Perform by parts Poomsae (4-6 steps) with weights strapped on belt.
KICKING DRILL (20 Workouts)
Pyramid Kicking (Normal/Resistance): Performed for front kick, turning kick, side kick. Resistance version involves band strapped to both ankles. This package consist of few components: (1) 6x kick raising (2) 6x kick setting (3) Single Kick Drill - LLow-RLow-LMed-RMed-LHigh-RHigh-LHigh-RHigh-LMed-RMed-LLow-RLow (4) Double Kick Drill - LLowHigh-RLowHigh x2 - LHighLow-RHighLow x2 (5) Triple Kick Drill (Rep 3) - LLowMedHigh-RLowMedHigh x2 - LHighMedLow-RHighMedLow x2 (6) Rep 5 above follows same format with more multiple kicks in midair
Taegeuk Kicks (Normal/Resistance): Resistance version involves band strapped to both ankles. Green - Step 1-2/3-4 Green Blue - Step 7-8 Blue - Step 15-16/17-18, Step 19-20 Blue Red - Step 5-6/11-12 Red - Step 14-16/17-19 Red Black - Step 3/19
Dan Kicks (Normal/Resistance): Resistance version involves band strapped to both ankles. Koryo - Step 1-2/5-6, Step 9-12, Step 16-18/21-23 Keumgang - Step 15/25 Taebaek - Step 9-13/14-18 Pyongwon - Step 4-6/13-15
Pistol Squat Jumping Front/Side: Jumping front - Perform pistol squat, jump and front kick with the standing leg Side - Perform pistol squat, side kick with the hanging leg
Lunges Kick: Perform a lunge and Front/Turning/Side kick everytime you stand.
4-Directions Performed for front kick, turning kick, side kick. This package consist of few components: (1) Single kick each direction (3 Sets) (2) Double kick each direction (2 Sets) (3) Triple kick each direction (1 Set)
Single Turning/Side End-Hold (Normal/Weighted/Resistance/Combined): Weighted version involves weights strapped to ankles. Resistance version involves band strapped to both ankles. Combined version involves weights and band strapped. With support from the wall, perform single turning/side kicks. Hold it at the peak position at the last rep.
Double Turning/Side Hold (Normal/Combined): Combined version involves weights and band strapped. With support from the wall, perform double turning/side kicks. Hold it at peak position every rep for 3 counts.
Slow Motion Turning/Side + Snap (Weighted/Resistance/Combined): Weighted version involves weights strapped to ankles. Resistance version involves band strapped to both ankles. Combined version involves weights and band strapped. With support from the wall, perform turning/side kick slowly (4 counts) and quick snap kick after reaching peak position.
Wall Side Kicks Perform side kick against the wall. Stand as close to the wall as possible and kick without hitting the wall. Best combo with wall splits.
Standard Workout Box
ARMS WORKOUT (15 Workouts) *Not all documented since some are self-explanatory
Archer Push-up Wide arm push-up to one side.
Diamond Push-up Thumb-Index for both hands form diamond shape on ground.
Push-up/Pull-up Hold Hold onto position at bottom of push-up without the chest touching the ground. Hold onto position at top of pull-up, try to keep chin above bar.
Chest Press Gym workout which requires flat bench. Square up the free weights to both side of body and push them up above (arms fully extended).
Shoulder Press Gym workout which requires inclined bench. Square up the free weights to both side of body and push them up above (arms fully extended).
Bench Press *Dangerous workout! Do not attempt alone if you are new. Gym workout which requires flat bench and barbell. Square the barbell such that it touches the chest and push it vertically up.
Bench Flys Gym workout which requires flat bench. With weights fully extended above chest, slowly open out to both sides and feel the stretch of the chest.
Pendulum Swings Gym workout which requires flat bench. Both hands hold up single weight above chest. Bring the weight behind your head all with arms fully extended.
Bicep Curls Free weights on each hand. Avoid using too much momentum to swing the weights up. Perform it controlled and slowly.
Overhead Tricep Extension Both hands hold up single weight above and behind head. Lower weight to back of neck level.
CORE WORKOUT (25 Workouts) *Not all documented since some are self-explanatory
Half Sit-ups Sit-up to halfway point. Halfway point to knee.
Single Side Sit-ups One leg folded up and supported on other knee. Right elbow reach for left knee. Left elbow reach for right knee.
Bicycle crunches Right elbow to left knee and left elbow to right knee while lying down. Perform it in rapid succession.
Jack Knives In leg raise holding position. Reach for the toes.
Russian Twist In seated position, hold onto a weight and bring across your body left and right while balancing on your tailbone.
Hanging Leg Raise Hang onto a pull-up bar and raise knee up in a tucking motion.
Hanging Side Crunch Similar to hanging leg raise, but knee sideward up.
Superman While lying on your belly, arms extend to the front. Bring up your chest and arms while raising your thighs and legs in this position. Abs Roller Requires an ab roller. With your knees on floor, hold on the the wheel and push until your body is at full stretch before returning.
Inclined Sit-ups Gym workout which requires inclined bench. Weighted version involves holding on a weight in front of chest while doing the exercise. Holding version involves body in half-suspended position.
RUNNING (3 Workouts)
Long Distance Jog Moderate-slow jog for long distances (>5km). Try to track with smartwatch or run on tracks.
Uncomfortable Pace Run at moderate-fast pace. You should feel slightly breathless while running.
Interval Sprints Sprint at full speed across short distances. If you are not breathless YOU ARE NOT RUNNING FAST ENOUGH!
